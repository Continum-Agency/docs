## What is Continum CLI?

Continum CLI is a command-line tool that prevents credential leaks before they reach your repository. It integrates seamlessly into your development workflow with a pre-commit hook that scans every commit for sensitive data.

## Two-Layer Protection

### Layer 1: Local Scanner (Instant)

The local scanner runs in milliseconds before your commit completes. It uses pattern matching to catch known credential types instantly.

- **Instant blocking** - Violations stop commits immediately
- **Works offline** - No network required for scanning
- **Zero latency** - Runs in milliseconds
- **Comprehensive patterns** - Detects API keys, tokens, credentials, and more

### Layer 2: Sandbox Audit (Background)

After your commit completes, a copy of the changes is sent to Continum for AI-powered analysis. This catches context-dependent violations that pattern matching might miss.

- **AI-powered detection** - Finds generic credentials with no known pattern
- **Context analysis** - Understands when data is sensitive based on usage
- **Fire-and-forget** - Doesn't block your workflow
- **Team visibility** - Creates signals for review in your dashboard

## How It Works

1. **Install** the CLI globally on your machine
2. **Login** to authenticate with your Continum account
3. **Initialize** in your project to install the pre-commit hook
4. **Commit normally** - scanning happens automatically

Every commit is scanned before it reaches your repository. Violations are caught instantly, and your team learns from every detection.
